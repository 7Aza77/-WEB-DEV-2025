Recipe #1

  • Time: 3 hours
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Kazy 0.7kg
Zhylky eti 1.5kg
Kartop 1kg
Piaz 0.8kg

In a medium-size pot place the meat, pour 3 L of water, add 1 TBSP of salt. Turn the heat on and bring the water to a slight boil.Once the foam starts forming, use a skimmer and remove the foam. At this time turn the heat down and keep simmering the broth.Start adding the flour, 1 cup at a time. Incorporate the first cup, add the second and see if you will need more.
